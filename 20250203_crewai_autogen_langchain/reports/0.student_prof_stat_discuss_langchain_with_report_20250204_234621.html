P-Value:** The p-value represents the probability of observing the data (or more extreme) assuming that there is no real effect or relationship between variables. In the context of statistical significance testing, Type I errors can lead to unnecessary waste of time and resources, while Type II errors might result in missed opportunities for valuable insights. **3.
